How We Can Help Jobseekers

Whether you’re looking for a temporary post to earn some extra money, some part-time work or even a new full-time position at Finding Worker we can help you do that and so much more. When you register as a candidate you can upload or build a CV on the website but we don’t just stop there!

The written word is certainly a powerful tool but some people are better using a visual medium. That’s why with Work Finder you can also upload your own photograph to your work profile.

By offering these more interactive features employers looking to find an employee or finding workers will be able to get a better idea of your skills. With the Work Finder you can also sign in and sign out when you get to/ leave work, this way you can ensure you’re always paid for the hours you work.

Work Finder also offers access to a fully automated online account. Work Finder also offers very competitive weekly pay that will be paid directly into your bank account.
